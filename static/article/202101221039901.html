Xiamen hosted 113 major events in 2020

Xiamen Commerce representatives announced last week that the city hosted in 2020 more than 3,200 conferences and a total of 113 major exhibitions such as CIFIT and the Straits Forum. 

The events generated a combined total of more than 17.6 billion yuan. 

New event introduced in the last year included the China International Advertising Festival and the China Educational Equipment Exhibition.
